Hi I'm Joey,

I am mix media artist, who devotes herself to creating things that are fun and fantastical. For as long as I remember art was always there in my world and brought me countless joys. I want to share and put my own art in the world to make others smile. I often fine myself wondering does art have to be serious? the answer is no and I am her to show "serious" art can also be fun and silly!
